Theme map

The arc is not just “old to new”. It is really a shift from labour and craft toward control, repeatability, hygiene, measurement, and finally sensors plus data.

  • Manual handling to mechanised throughput
  • Container choice to flavour / oxygen management
  • Wild process variability to monitored fermentation
  • Cellar intuition to analytical instrumentation
  • Standalone machinery to connected data systems
