Users select a soil (sand, loam, clay, sandy loam, clay loam), adjust rainfall intensity, and watch an animated cross-section showing wetting front penetration, saturated zones, field capacity zones, surface ponding, and runoff. Built on a simplified Green-Ampt infiltration model with realistic USDA soil parameters.
