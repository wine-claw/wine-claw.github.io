What the app is doing

  • ETc = Kc × ETo
  • Total available water = AWC × root depth
  • Trigger line = total available water × (1 - MAD)
  • Gross refill = deficit ÷ irrigation efficiency

This is a simplified educational planner for comparing irrigation scenarios in vineyards. It does not include rainfall, salinity, vine variety, row orientation, block heterogeneity, soil probes, water quality, or validated local crop coefficients.
