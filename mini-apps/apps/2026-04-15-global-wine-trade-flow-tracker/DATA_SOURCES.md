### Key insights from OIV 2024 report
- Global wine export volume: 100.2 million hectolitres (+0.8%)
- Global wine export value: €36.038 billion (-0.5%)
- Bulk wine: 34.4M hL (+3.9%), only category with value growth (+9.3%)
- Sparkling: 10% of volume, ~25% of revenue; avg €7.90/L
- Bottled still: >50% of volume, >67% of revenue; avg €4.76/L
- Bulk: >33% of volume, only 7% of revenue; avg €0.77/L

### France export details (€, 2024)
- Total: €11.7 billion from <13M hL
- Avg price: ~€7.90/L (highest among top exporters)
- Top destinations: USA, UK, Germany, Belgium, China, Japan, Switzerland

### Italy export details (€, 2024)
- Total: €8.136 billion from 21.7M hL
- Avg price: €3.74/L
- Top destinations: USA, UK, Germany

### Spain export details (€, 2024)
- Total: ~€3 billion from 20M hL (-4.5% volume)
- Value +1.6%, avg price +6.4%
- Top destinations: UK, Germany, USA, France

### China import market dynamics 2024
- Total: US$1.592B (+37.2%)
- Australia recovers from 2020 tariffs: from 1.6M L to 78.6M L
- France + Australia = 68% of total import spending
- Chile still #1 by volume (94M L) but -15%
- Wine consumption in China is 1/3 of what it was 5 years ago
